description: >-
  A glycolic peel is a chemical peel that uses glycolic acid to remove the top
  layers of skin. Glycolic acid is a type of alpha hydroxy acid (AHA). It is a
  naturally occurring chemical that is found in sugar cane. Glycolic acid is
  used to treat a variety of skin conditions, including acne, wrinkles, and
  uneven skin tone. Glycolic peels are also used to treat precancerous skin
  growths and skin damage caused by the sun.
