  A pyruvic peel is a chemical peel that uses pyruvic acid to remove the top
  layers of skin. Pyruvic acid is a type of alpha hydroxy acid (AHA). It is a
  naturally occurring chemical that is found in apples. Pyruvic acid is used to
  treat a variety of skin conditions, including acne, wrinkles, and uneven skin
  tone. Pyruvic peels are also used to treat precancerous skin growths and skin
  damage caused by the sun.
